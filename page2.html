Enjoy Today's Interesting Facts

  • Tabby is not a breed: Tabby is a pattern of markings on a cat’s body, and almost every cat breed has members that are considered tabby cats
  • Tabby cats have the letter M on their forehead: A tabby cat’s defining characteristic is the letter M’s presence on the cat’s forehead between the ears. They will also have stripes by the eyes and across the cheeks
